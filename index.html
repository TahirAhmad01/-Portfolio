About me

Who am i
Hi there everyone this is Ruhul Amin.
I’m an Android Developer, passionate about building great apps.
For me app development is not just about coding, it’s also about creating an awesome user experience.
So far, I have worked on almost 8+ apps. Most of these apps were developed while I was studing at diploma in engineering .
In my portfolio, I am only showcasing apps that I have personally designed and developed. I have done Diploma in Computer Engineering from Rajshahi Polytechnic Institute.
 I started my career when i was a engineering student. where I tryed to learn about programming , Pythan, C# .
 Then i finded the love in Java Programming and i got a chance to join a skill development training which is called
(Mobile Game and Application Development) which is given by ICT Division of Bangladesh .
 Later on, I decided to get into Android development while working at a startup. That’s when I learnt about Android MVP, UX/UI, how to scope out an app, importance of analytics etc. Currently, I am getting into iOS app development as well.
 I have created more than 5 Android apps on Play store. In this process,
I realized that well-written code always comes back to help you.
I cannot stress the importance of efficient, scalable and reusable code.
It saved a lot of my and my team’s time.
Writing good code comes out of practice, experience and lots of code reading.
A good UI/UX design is also the key to your app’s success.
Design should be geared towards making the app easy to use for any random user.
It should also be practical enough for developers to implement quickly.
I always look at the design components available in Material Design before creating a new one.
 Note: If you want to know the cost of building an mobile app, my contact info is listed at the bottom.
I specialize in building high quality and cost effective Android apps.
Apart from Android development, I have helped people in identifying customers,
figuring out business model and also with non-engineering startup related stuff.
Keep reading to see some of my existing apps.
